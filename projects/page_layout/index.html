What is beyblade?

Beyblade is a kid's toy created by Takara Tomy. It has span multiple anime franchises and has been one of the best selling kid's toy to date. Beyblade consists of a launcher, ripcord, and the main bey. You play it by attaching the bey to the launcher, then pulling the ripcord to spin the bey.You win buy knocking the enemy bey out of the stadium, or by making the bey burst into pieces.

The most intriguing part of beyblade is its customization. You can mix and match parts creating the ultimate beyblade. A bey's parts consists of a layer, a disk, and a driver. By picking out the best combinations of parts can lead you into victory.
