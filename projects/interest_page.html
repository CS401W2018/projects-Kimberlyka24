Interest - Types of Mechanical Keyboard Switches

Interest Hero

Linear Switches

A linear switch is the simplest type of mechanical keyboard switch. When you press a linear switch, there is no clicking sound and no feedback. This type of switch is used for minimalists, especially the ones who don't want to disturb others while typing.

Tactile Switches

A tactile switch is a switch that when clicked, makes a noticeable feedback. This feedback lets the user know that the key is registered. It doesn't create a noticeable sound, making it good for those who want to feel the key registered without disturbing others.

Clicky Switches

A clicky switch is a switch that aside from providing a feedback when clicked, it also creates a noticeable sound. Clicky switches are usually the loudest types of mechanical keyboard switches that may annoy others as you type.
