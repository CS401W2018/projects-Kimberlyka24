Hero - Walter L. Cherry (Cherry Corporation)

Interest Hero

Walter L. Cherry is the founder of the Cherry Electrical Products Corporation (now known as Cherry Corporation). His company started with making microswitches for machines like an arcade machine. Around 1973, they started manufacturing computer keyboards, together with patenting the first keyboard switch. Around 1983, the company started the lineup of the MX key switches. Now, it is more commonly known as the Cherry MX switches. Walter is a hero of sorts because if he did not founded the Cherry Corporation, we may never have these awesome keyboard switches.

The start of the cherries
Walter L. Cherry started his company in 1953. He set up his company in a Highland Park basement.
Age when he started and when he retired
He was 36 years old when he started the company. He retired around 1994 at the age of 77.
Death
He died in 1996 at the age of 79.
