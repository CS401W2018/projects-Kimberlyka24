THE HISTORY OF BEYBLADE

Beyblade is a popular franchise today. It is played by kids and adults alike. But how did it started? Beyblade is inspired from a traditional Japanese spinning top game called "Beigoma." The popularity of the beyblade was boosted thanks to the anime. The anime is consisted of Beyblade, Beyblade V-Force, Beyblade G-Revolution, Beyblade Metal Fusion, Beyblade Metal Masters, Beyblade Metal Fury, Beyblade Shogun Steel, Beyblade Burst, Beyblade Burst Evolution, Beyblade Burst Turbo, Beyblade Burst Rise, Beyblade Burst Surge, Beyblade Burst QuadDrive, Beyblade Burst QuadStrike, and the latest one is Beyblade X.

Takara Tomy is the manufacturer in Japan while Hasbro has made it possible for beyblade to be bought throughout the entire world. With also rumors of the Beyblade founders wanting to join the olympics, its popularity among old players and new ones are starting to reignite.
