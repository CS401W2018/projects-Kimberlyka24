BEYBLADE LIST

Here are the some of the latest beyblades you can buy in 2024. Each has its own strengths and weaknesses.

PHOENIX WING

Phoenix Wing is considered as the fastest beyblade to date in the series Beyblade X. It is used by the character Blaze Fujiwara in the anime. To date, it is the heaviest attack type in Beyblade X. For more description, click here.

WIZARD ROD

Wizard Rod is one of the strongest stamina type beyblade. It is used by the character Multi Nanairo in the anime Beyblade X. In tournaments, it is one of the most used beyblade. This bey can outspin most of its opponents. For more description, click here.

KNIFE SHINOBI

Knife Shinobi is one of the tricky beys that you can use. It is a defense type used by Quinn Manju. The blades are shaped like a kunai. The one in the photo is the recolored version bundled together with Beyblade X XONE Nintendo Switch game. For more description, click here.
