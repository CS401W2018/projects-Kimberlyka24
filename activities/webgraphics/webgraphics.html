Web Graphics

What kind of photos are good to encode using JPEG?

JPEG is best for photos that are complex and has many colors

What kind of photos are good to encode using GIF?

GIF is ideal for simple graphics with animations and limited colors

What is unique about the PNG format?

PNG is good for lossless compression as well as transparent backgrounds

How do you resize your photo to the size it should be displayed?

Use an editing software, or use css to change the image size

Why should you resize photos using photo editing software instead of resizing it using CSS?

It ensures better compression quality and may avoid browser scaling issues
