  1. The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do?

    It specifies the URL or endpoint of the data on the form where it will be sent when it is submitted.

  2. The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do?

    It specifies the method that should be used when submitting the data on the form to the server.

  3. What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"?

    The main difference of the two is that GET sends data in the URL, making it visible, while POST sends data in the background, making it hidden.
